We are a rescue dedicated to improving the lives of pets in New Mexico

Bosque Buddies was founded in 2024 to address a growing need for animal care and advocacy in the state. We are a foster-based rescue and all of our animals are in safe, loving foster homes where they can learn to love and be loved in return.
